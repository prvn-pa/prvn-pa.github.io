Hi, I am Praveen.

An Assistant Research Scientist.

Working at the Topological and Two-dimensional Materials Group, Beijing Academy of Quantum Information Sciences, Beijing, China, I primarily focusing on the design, development and analysis of molecular materials for photonic applications.

My present work focuses on the development of long sought organic lasers using oligothiophenes as the active medium. We're tailoring this system by using density functional theory and explore the potential derivatives experimentally.
